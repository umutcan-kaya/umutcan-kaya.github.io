I’m a PhD student in set theory at the University of Warsaw and IMPAN (supervisor: Grigor Sargsyan). My research interests lies in the intersection of descriptive set theory and inner model theory. My research explores the connections between inner model theory and large cardinals. I am especially interested in how generic absoluteness can help to build a strong and rich theory of sets.
